# battleship-game

Battleship game built from scratch and improved in different versions. Built as a series of challenges in 'Head First JS Programming' book.

## To Play: 
Open the game here: https://mashablair.github.io/battleship-game/

Enter your guesses by entering the 2-character guess in this format: e.g. "A1" or "D4" using the letters and numbers on the board. 

Click 'Fire!' button or hit 'Enter'.  

There are 3 battleships hidden on the board by the computer. Your goal is to find those battleships and sink them all. Watch for the messages in the upper-left corner that provide guidance and status of the game. 

Enjoy!
